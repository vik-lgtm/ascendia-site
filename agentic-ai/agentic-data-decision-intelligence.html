Agentic Data & Decision Intelligence

We help businesses move from static dashboards to AI-powered decision systems. Using Google Cloud, BigQuery, Looker and Gemini, we build data platforms that let leaders ask natural-language questions, identify trends, detect inconsistencies, generate forecasts and receive decision-ready insights.

What it is

Agentic Data & Decision Intelligence.

Dashboards show what happened; a decision system tells you what to do about it. On a BigQuery and Looker foundation with a Gemini layer on top, leaders ask questions in plain language, get forecasts and alerts, and act on insight the same day the data lands.

Business impact

  • Reduce dependency on manual reporting
